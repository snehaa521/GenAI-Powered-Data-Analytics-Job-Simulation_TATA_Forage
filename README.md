# GenAI-Powered-Data-Analytics-Job-Simulation_TATA_Forage

## Overview
This repository contains my work from Tata’s GenAI Powered Data Analytics Job Simulation on the Forage platform.  
In this project, I analyzed customer data, identified risks of credit delinquency, and developed actionable, AI-driven strategies to help decision-makers reduce risk.  

I used GenAI tools like ChatGPT and Google Gemini to assist with data analysis, predictive modeling, and building a responsible AI strategy. This simulation gave me hands-on experience combining analytics with practical business strategy.

---

## What I Worked On
- Explored the dataset to find patterns, anomalies, and missing values.  
- Created a conceptual predictive model for delinquency risk.  
- Developed stakeholder-ready insights and SMART business recommendations.  
- Designed a responsible AI-powered collections system that balances automation, fairness, and explainability.
